Cookie Policy

Last updated: 23 June 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how the I.N.E.S. Cakes website uses cookies and similar local-storage technologies, in line with the EU ePrivacy rules and the Irish Data Protection Act 2018.

1. What we use

We keep this site simple. We do not use advertising or third-party tracking cookies. We only use a small amount of essential local storage in your browser to make the site work:

  • ines-cart - remembers the items in your basket.
  • ines-my-orders - remembers order codes on your device so you can track them.
  • ines-session - used only in the private admin area for the bakery owner.

This information stays on your device, is strictly necessary for the features you use, and is not used to track you across other websites.

2. Third-party services

The site loads Google Fonts and connects to Google Firebase (database) and Vercel (hosting) to function. These providers may set technical cookies necessary for delivery and security. See our Privacy Policy for details on data processors.

3. Managing cookies and local storage

Because we only use strictly necessary storage, no consent banner is required for tracking. You can clear cookies and local storage at any time through your browser settings; doing so will reset your basket, saved order codes and language preference.

Questions? Email orders@inescake.com.
