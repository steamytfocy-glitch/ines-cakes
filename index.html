About Us

Welcome to I.N.E.S. - a home bakery in the heart of Donegal, Ireland. Every cake we create is made with passion, using only natural and high-quality ingredients.

We believe that a cake is more than just a dessert - it's the centrepiece of your celebration. Whether it's a birthday, wedding, anniversary, or just a sweet Friday treat, we'll make it special.

All cakes are baked fresh to order. We offer a wide range of flavours and designs - from classic elegance to bold custom creations based on your ideas.
